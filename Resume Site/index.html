About Me

I am a creative, vibrant, and positive individual who believes deeply in the power of honesty, teamwork, and lifelong learning. My journey has been shaped by a combination of diverse experiences and a strong set of values that guide me both personally and professionally. I approach every challenge with enthusiasm, an open mind, and a commitment to continuous improvement, always striving to learn from others—especially those with more experience—and contribute meaningfully to any team or project.

While working as a Junior Web Developer at Gelmar, I’ve had the opportunity to dive into the world of web development and user experience optimization, mastering technologies like Adobe Magento 2 along the way. This role has reinforced my belief in the importance of delivering value through creativity and problem-solving. Prior to this, my roles as a sports coach and store manager in my dad’s bicycle shop taught me the significance of patience, leadership, and maintaining a positive attitude even in challenging situations. These experiences have instilled in me a deep appreciation for collaboration, attention to detail, and the joy of helping others grow.

Beyond my professional life, I’m a music lover who enjoys spending time with friends playing sports and engaging in activities that keep me active and connected. Creativity is at the core of everything I do, whether it's solving complex problems or finding innovative ways to improve processes. I believe that being honest, authentic, and supportive fosters trust and productivity, which are essential in any environment.

Now, I’m eager to take the next step in my career and explore new areas within the IT industry, such as software development and full-stack development. I thrive in dynamic environments where I can quickly pick up new skills, embrace challenges, and embark on exciting adventures. With a proven ability to adapt and learn rapidly, I’m confident in my capacity to make meaningful contributions to any organization while continuing to grow as a developer and as a person.

In summary, I’m not just looking for a job—I’m seeking a new chapter filled with opportunities to expand my knowledge, collaborate with talented professionals, and push the boundaries of what I can achieve. If you’re looking for someone who brings energy, curiosity, and a passion for excellence, I’d be thrilled to join your team.
